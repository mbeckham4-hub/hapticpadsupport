# hapticpadsupport
Support for HapticPad.
Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Overview

HapticPad is designed with privacy as a core feature. We believe your data should stay yours—so we don’t collect any personal data at all.

2. Data Collection

HapticPad does not collect, store, track, or share any personal information.

That includes:

* No names
* No email addresses
* No location data
* No usage analytics
* No device identifiers
* No app activity tracking
* No third-party tracking tools

If it’s personal, we don’t touch it.

3. How the App Works

All features in HapticPad run locally on your device. Any inputs you provide stay on your device and are not transmitted anywhere.

Think of it like a notebook that never leaves your hands.

4. Third-Party Services

HapticPad does not use third-party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.

5. Data Sharing

Since we don’t collect any data, there is nothing to share, sell, or transfer.

6. Security

Because no user data is stored or transmitted, your information remains safe by default. There’s nothing sitting on servers waiting to be breached.

7. Children’s Privacy

HapticPad does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is safe to use without any data concerns.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, it will be updated here. However, our core promise—no data collection—will not change.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer through the app’s support channel (if available).
